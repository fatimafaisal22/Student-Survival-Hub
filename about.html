About

Student Survival Hub is a simple website designed to help students with useful tips on studying, managing time, handling stress, and planning their careers.

This project is created using HTML and CSS with a little JavaScript for interactivity.

Our goal is to provide easy-to-understand advice to support students on their academic journey.
