Stress Management Tips

Learn simple ways to stay calm and focused during your studies.

Practice Deep Breathing

Deep breathing helps calm the mind and reduce stress. It improves focus and relaxes the body during stressful situations.

Stay Active

Regular physical activity helps reduce stress and improve mood. Exercise keeps the body healthy and increases energy levels.

Get Enough Sleep

Getting enough sleep helps the brain and body recover. Proper rest improves concentration, memory, and overall well-being.

Talk to Someone

Talking to someone helps release stress and emotional pressure. Sharing problems with friends or family can make you feel supported and relaxed.
